I'm a PhD student in Electrical Engineering Department and Princeton Neuroscience Institute at Princeton University working with Prof. Peter Ramadge. My research interests include Machine Learning and Computational Neuroscience. My current focus is on handling inter-subject variablitliy to utilize multi-subject human brain data with a hope to learn more about how the brain works. Currently we can decode an individual's brain response based on other subjects' data. Besdies, I have also worked on several theoretical/applied machine learning projects in various domains at Princeton, Amazon, and Palantir.
